GoHelp

Made By Innovative Teens Perceiving To Help Others

Description

GoHelp is an innovative news app that uses image and text recognition software to deliver relevant news stories directly to the client. This is possible when the client uses tthe app to take a picture of anything, anywhere, GoHelp provides an exceptionally easy and accessible experience to view news. With GoHelp, the world is your news feed, map, and more.

Strategy

People around the world can now attain so many different perspectives on whatever they come upon. GoHelp gives everyone an amazing opportunity to explore their desires from any photo provided!

Explore

Once the application presents the relevant news stories, the client then has the freedom to explore related articles and discover more about the subject.

Direction

While our app implements several of the core ideas that we imagined for this application, many of the features had to be left out due to time constraints. Some of the features that are planned for development are filters based on type of news, user accounts, and more.

Expertise
